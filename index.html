Mechanic Finder

MechanicFinder is an innovative mobile app to assist you in finding a mechanic that suits you based on what you want and need. With a directory, rating, reviews and rewards database and some help from a handful of clever features, you'll wonder how you've ever serviced your car without it.

MechanicFinder allows you to search between mechanics by location, price, and rating but also features advanced search options like European and performance servicing, available loan cars and which businesses are RAA or warranty approved, plus much more!

MechanicFinder will even send a notification to your phone when your next service is due or your vehicle is ready to pick up. As well as saving a detailed online copy of your vehicles service history in your MechanicFinder account. Let's not forget the service points, a rewards points system that notifies you know when your mechanic is offering deals such as car washing, discounted servicing, cheap tyres, or anything at all!

MechanicFinder makes servicing your car faster, easier and more rewarding than ever before.
